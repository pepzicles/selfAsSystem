How to visualize flavor profile:


1. Get 100ml of a certain solution.

2. If the food is solid, crush the food into crumb-like pieces as small as possible, and try to dissolve them in 100ml of water.

3. Insert clean pH, salinity probes into the mixture.

4. Wait at least 10s.

5. Observe results on the radar chart!
